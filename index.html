Bibliography
Yuanshuai Zheng received the B.S. degree in Network Engineering from University of Electronic Science and Technology of China, Chengdu, China, in 2020. He is currently pursuing the Ph.D. degree with the School of Science and Engineering and the Shenzhen Future Network of Intelligence Institute (FNii-Shenzhen) at The Chinese University of Hong Kong, Shenzhen, Guangdong, China. His research interests include dynamic 3D communications, UAV placement and trajectory optimization, geography-aware air-to-ground channel modeling, radio map, and MIMO channel estimation.
